Pixel Patrol - Social Engineering Awareness

Demonstrating real-world detection of harmful and deceptive content online

This page showcases a series of educational video demonstrations that explore how users may encounter harmful or deceptive content on the internet, particularly through social engineering attacks. The videos highlight browsing behavior from a typical user’s perspective, recorded during tests conducted using the Firefox browser.

The goal of these demonstrations is to emphasize the effectiveness of the Pixel Patrol Firefox extension in proactively detecting and warning users about potentially harmful pages and misleading ads.

The video demonstrations are divided into two categories:

  • Before Installing Pixel Patrol: Browsing without any protection or content scanning in place.
  • After Installing Pixel Patrol: The extension actively flags and warns users about detected threats in real time.
