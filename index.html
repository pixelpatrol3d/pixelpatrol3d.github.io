Pixel Patrol 3D (PP3D)

Real-Time Defense Against Deceptive Web Attacks

This page presents a real-user perspective walkthrough showcasing the impact of Pixel Patrol 3D, a browser-based defense framework designed to detect Behavior Manipulation Attacks (BMAs) in real time.

BMAs exploit persuasive visuals and misleading text to manipulate users into unsafe actions—like downloading fake software, enabling notifications, or calling fake support numbers. These threats are difficult to block with traditional URL filters.

Our browser extension, uses a privacy-preserving on-device model to scan web content using screenshots and OCR. It flags malicious pages and proactively warns users before any harm occurs.

The following demonstrations are divided into two parts:

  • Scenario 1: Browsing Without Protection – Highlights how deceptive pages appear when unprotected.
  • Scenario 2: Real-Time Protection With Pixel Patrol – Shows the extension actively blocking harmful content.
